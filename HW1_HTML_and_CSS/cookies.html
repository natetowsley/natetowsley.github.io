What are "Cookies"?

Cookies or "session cookies" are temporary text files that a website installs on to your device temporarily while you visit their website. These files allow the website to monitor your activity on their website, and can improve your as well as others' experience in the future. The most common example of session cookies are used to track your cart when shopping online as you browse different pages on the website. When you visit a website, the cookie is generated as well as the "session ID". Both of these are only accessible to the server that made them.
