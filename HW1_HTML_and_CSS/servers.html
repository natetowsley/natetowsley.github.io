What are Servers?

Servers, in a nutshell, are devices/programs that are used to serve other devices. Similarly to the experience you have in a restaraunt, you request certain food, and your server brings it to you. However, servers come in many shapes and sizes. Some servers are dedicated, large data centers that serve only to provide for other devices. On the other hand, your device can also become a server for other devices. Your device can even be botht the server and the client for various programs on your device communicating with each other. These servers work by using the client server model, seen on the home page. The server's job, for the most part, is to fulfill requests from the client.
